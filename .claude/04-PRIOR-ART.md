## Current truth (summary, 2026-06-21)

- **Quartz v5** beats our own Starlight prototype on OFM fidelity and is free — forward publishing is a commoditized, dead frame for v1.
- **But the contribution surface is publicly unclaimed**: Quartz's maintainer closed the web-editor/CMS requests as out of scope. The reverse web→vault round-trip is structurally impossible in any SSG. That is the differentiator.
- Obsidian Publish: read-only, $10/mo, proprietary CDN (the lock-in failure mode Principle 1 exists to prevent).
- Decap/Sveltia Open Authoring is the strongest zero-git contribution pattern; all git-backed CMSs still require an account or invite as of mid-2026 (the serverless contribution-bot pattern is the zero-account path being evaluated).
